Halasana Or Plough Pose

Technique: Lie straight on the ground. keep the palms along the body and put them straight on the ground. Slowly lift the lower part of the body i.e. feet in a rhythm and touch the ground behind the head with the toes. Stay in this position for 10-15 seconds and then slowly come back to the initial position.

Benefits

  • Improves Flexibility
  • Strengthens Abdominal Muscles
  • Thyroid Stimulation
  • Alleviates Stress
  • Enhances Digestion
  • Relieves Backache
  • Improves Posture
  • Boosts Blood Circulation
  • Strengthens Shoulders and Neck
  • Reduces Fatigue
  • Aids in Weight Management
