Social Anxiety Disorder

Social Anxiety, also known as social phobia, is characterized by an intense fear of social situations and a strong desire to avoid them. Individuals with social anxiety may be afraid of being judged or embarrassed in front of others, leading to avoidance of social interactions.

How To Avoid It

  • Therapy
  • Gradual Exposure
  • Mindfulness and Relaxation Techniques
  • Physical Health
  • Social Skills Training
